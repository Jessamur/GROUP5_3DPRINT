OUR SERVICES

Here at resin8, we believe in making. We offer a variety of services to makers of all levels, from computer-aided design (CAD), 3D modeling and printing onsite, to hands-on classes and guides. Read on about our services below and contact us with any questions.

Design & Printing

Come in with your design all ready to load into our printers or reserve time at one of our CAD or 365-degree 3D modeling scan stations to design onsite prior to printing.

The resin8 makerspace has 9 printers available for use:

• 2 Ultimaker s5
• 4 Makerbot Replicators
• 3 FlashForge Adventurers

Creality CR-T 3D Scanner for high-quality 3D modeling

We have 4 fully equipped CAD stations loaded with:

• Adobe Creative Suite
• MakerBot CloudPrint
• Autodesk Fusion 360

Materials

Choose from a menu of high-quality resin filaments, liquids and powders to bring your project to life. Already have your own materials? Feel free to bring them, but check with us first to make sure they're compatible with our printers.

Contact us for material types and rates. They may vary based on availability and supply.

Classes & Lessons

3D printing is both an art and a science. Our expert maker staff is here to help you learn and refine the process. We offer group classes in 6-week intervals for all levels of experience, and private guidance on demand.

Class offerings:

• Beginning Design & Modeling
• Advanced Design & Modeling
• Intro to 3D printing
• Advanced 3D Printing
• How to Make Action Figures

Contact us for class schedules and rates.
