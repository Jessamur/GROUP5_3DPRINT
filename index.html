MAKE YOUR DREAMS 3D!

Immerse yourself in the art of 3D printing at Resin8, a full-service 3D printing makerspace that welcomes printers of all ages and abilities.

Welcome to Resin8. We are obsessed with 3D printing, and want to share that love with the world! Whether you're looking to start a new hobby, or a professional in need of a prototype for your business, come visit our makerspace to fulfill your 3D printing goals. We can't wait to let you into our world where creativity and imagination come to life every day.

Visitors can use our 9 state-of-the-art printers, dream up new creations using our design lab, turn yourself into an action figure (yes, really!) or join us for regularly scheduled hands-on classes and private lessons to hone your skill as a maker.

Learn more about our services, or contact us for rates and schedules.
